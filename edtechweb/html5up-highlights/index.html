Let's Find One!

This activity will take place outside. Our high school is fortunate to have several American Chestnuts on campus. Let's learn what they look like and then find some!

Leaf Characteristics

The American Chestnut has characteristic boat-shaped leaves and its outline is laced with teeth. Despite this fairly unique shape, it can be mistaken for lookalikes. View this quick identification sheet that can help you distinguish the American Chestnut.

Next

Dive in Deeper

Do you love tree identification? This presentation will show you more characteristics of the American Chestnut and help you identify the tree with confidence.

Found one? Share
