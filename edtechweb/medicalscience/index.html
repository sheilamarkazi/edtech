Healthcare Career Pathways

Wytheville Community College (WCC) offers 15 pathways in the form of degrees and certificates that allow you to get jobs in the healthcare industry.

Your task today is to decide your opinion on which pathway(s) might be good options for you, and which pathways you do not want to take.

How do you decide if a pathway is a good fit for you? You can decide based on factors such as:

  • Which jobs are the most interesting to you? You might find certain healthcare career pathways might be more fun or interesting than others.
  • Which jobs makes enough money for the lifestyle you want to live? Different degrees/certificates lead to earning different salaries.
  • How long does it take to complete the necessary degree or certificate? You can meet the qualifications for some jobs by just completing a certificate! Some jobs require a two-year degree that you can earn at WCC. There are other jobs that you can start preparing for at WCC, and then you can finish your degree at a four-year college or university.


Please keep track of your thoughts and considerations in this Google Spreadsheet. Your teacher will use the Grading Rubric to evaluate your spreadsheet.
